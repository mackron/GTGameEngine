100% free and open source.

Released under the MIT Licence for true freedom. Go commercial, fork it, do whatever you want. And to top it off, it's all hosted on GitHub.

Built-in editing tools.

Every game has the engine's editing tools built right in. Just hit Shift+Tab and enter the editing universe. Start editing whenever you want.

Cross platform. Kind of.

Works on both Windows and Linux. No support for phones, but here's the spin: it's built for PC. No compromises.
